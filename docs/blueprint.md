# **App Name**: Visionary Ventures

## Core Features:

- Business Plan Outline Generation: Generate a business plan outline based on user input, incorporating industry-standard sections such as executive summary, market analysis, competitive analysis, and financial projections.
- RAG-based Information Retrieval: A tool to retrieve relevant information from uploaded documents (including text, images, and videos related to the business) to inform the business plan generation. Includes capabilities similar to NotebookLM to facilitate linking supporting documents.
- Financial Modeling and Forecasting: Generates basic financial statements and financial ratios and presents them in charts, incorporating API-sourced price data, estimated expenses (salary, utilities, materials, rent), and allows for what-if analysis over various time periods (6, 12, 24 months). Also takes inflation into consideration, includes an estimation of the number of customers you need to serve in Puerto Rico to break even.
- Template Library: Provide a library of customizable business plan templates for various industries and business models. Uses the provided information to suggest which template the user may prefer. Can handle up to 50-page documents. Should support integration of 'Guía de Recursos Estándar para Planes y Modelos de Negocio' information in its output as a tool, upon user request.
- Image, Mindmap, and Diagram Generation: Generates images, mindmaps, diagrams of key business concepts and models, based on text descriptions from the user. Also includes the "CAFES UNISON" logo as part of the images generated by the AI.
- Video Overview Generation: Generate a video overview of the business plan with automated narration.
- Interactive Dashboard: Display key business metrics, charts, and reports in an interactive dashboard, to aid strategic decision making.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep teal (#008080) to evoke feelings of trust, growth, and sophistication.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC) to create a warm, inviting, and neutral canvas.
- Accent color: Muted gold (#B8860B) to signify value, highlight important elements, and provide contrast against the teal background.
- Additional colors: Use a combination of purple and yellow from the developer's logo for accents and highlights.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' serif font to provide a sophisticated, elegant feel. Body text: 'PT Sans' sans-serif font for improved readability in smaller text sizes.
- Use professional and clean icons that are relevant to business and finance.
- Maintain a clean, well-organized, and intuitive layout for easy navigation and information access.
- Incorporate subtle animations for loading screens and transitions to provide a smooth user experience.
